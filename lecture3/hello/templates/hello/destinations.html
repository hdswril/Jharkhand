Hundru Falls

Hundru Falls is one of the highest waterfalls in Jharkhand, cascading from a height of around 98 meters. It is located near Ranchi and is a popular spot for picnics and nature lovers. The surrounding forest adds to its serene beauty.

Best Time to Visit: Monsoon and post-monsoon months (July to October).

Betla National Park

Betla National Park is part of the Palamau Tiger Reserve and offers a rich experience of biodiversity. Tourists can spot elephants, deer, and various species of birds. It is an excellent destination for wildlife enthusiasts and eco-tourism.

Best Time to Visit: November to February.

Deoghar (Baba Baidyanath Dham)

Deoghar is a sacred pilgrimage town famous for the Baba Baidyanath Temple, one of the 12 Jyotirlingas. Visitors come here to seek blessings and participate in religious rituals. The town is surrounded by lush greenery and peaceful hills.

Best Time to Visit: Shravan month (July-August) and winter months.

Netarhat

Netarhat, known as the 'Queen of Chotanagpur', is a hill station offering breathtaking sunrise and sunset views. It's a calm retreat with dense forests and waterfalls nearby, making it a perfect getaway for nature lovers.

Best Time to Visit: October to March.

IIT (ISM) Dhanbad

IIT (ISM) Dhanbad is a premier public technical and research university in Dhanbad, India. Founded in 1926 as the Indian School of Mines to train in mining and geology, it was upgraded to an IIT in 2016.

Best Time to Visit: All year round.
